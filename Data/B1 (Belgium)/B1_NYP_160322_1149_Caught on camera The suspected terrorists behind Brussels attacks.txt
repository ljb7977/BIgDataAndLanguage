Surveillance cameras captured images of three suspected terrorists in the moments before the twin blasts at the Brussels airport, according to a report.
The men can be seen pushing baggage carts at Zaventem Airport, where 14 people were killed in the attacks, dhnet.be reported. A Kalashnikov assault rifle was discovered at the site.
Sources told the site that the two men on the left, who are seen wearing gloves, were hiding the detonators for suicide bombs.
The man on the right is being sought by authorities.
